What we are modelling?

We are modelling a car insurance quote model. This will be a python program that prompts the user to enter information about their vehicle, package, driving experience etc. Our program will not have a GUI(Graphical User Interface), it will strictly take input through the command line. Upon taking input our program will calculate and output a quote to the user based on the variables they entered.

What research we carried out?

We looked at different car insurance providers' website to get an idea of how much quotes were for different people, depending on the unique factors. We compiled all variables we found on these websites into our own variable table and then we chose what we wanted to include. We based some of our percentages off of research, however we could not access the actual rates insurance companies use to calculate premiums. We found most of our rates through trial and error, trying to keep the quotes as realistic as possible. To ensure the quotes remained realistic, we compared our programs output to real life premiums and adjusted our figures accordingly.
