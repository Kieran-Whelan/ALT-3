What we are modelling?

We are modelling a car insurance quote model. This will be a python program that prompts the user to enter information about their vehicle, package, driving experience etc. Our program will not have a GUI(Graphical User Interface), it will strictly take input through the command line.

Why we chose this project?

We chose this project as we are both new drivers trying to find a reasonable insurance quote. We are also both interested in maths. One of us wants to do Actuarial Maths in College, which looks at insurance quotes and risk assessment.

What research we carried out?

We looked at different car insurance providers' website to get an idea of how much quotes were for different people, depending on the unique factors
