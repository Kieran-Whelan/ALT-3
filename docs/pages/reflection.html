Summary

Our goal was to investigate if the quarter of a year affected consumer spending. We investigated this by plotting of line graph of all three categories and a graph of the median values for each quarter. We found out that Q3 is the peak time for hotels and restaurants, Q4 is the peek time for Food, Clothing and Footwear and Q4 in general is the peak time period for consumer spending each year. Lastly, consumers spend the least amount of money in Q1. The min value for all full(1997 - 2017) graphs was 1997 Q1 and the max was 2017 Q4 this is inline with UK inflation trends over the last 20 years.
